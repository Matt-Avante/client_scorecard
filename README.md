# Avante Quarterly Business Check-in

A client-facing Business Check-in designed to help Avante Media better understand each client's business, priorities and feedback ahead of quarterly review meetings.

Rather than acting as a traditional satisfaction survey, this platform is designed to create better conversations by giving clients an opportunity to share what's happening in their business, highlight opportunities and challenges, and provide honest feedback on the support they receive from Avante.

---

## Purpose

The Quarterly Business Check-in helps us:

- Understand how our clients' businesses are performing
- Identify opportunities and challenges earlier
- Improve marketing strategy using client feedback
- Prepare more valuable quarterly review meetings
- Measure client satisfaction and account health
- Identify referral and testimonial opportunities

The aim is to move beyond campaign reporting and become a more proactive growth partner.

## Response Collection

Responses can be collected directly into Google Sheets using Google Apps Script or through Tally, Make, Zapier or Notion.

Each question has a matching field name to make integrations straightforward and ensure reporting remains consistent across all platforms.
